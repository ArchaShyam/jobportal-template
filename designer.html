Graphic Designer

Kannur,Kerala

Part-time

Job description

We are looking for a creative and skilled Graphic Designer to join our team! As a Graphic Designer, you will be responsible for creating eye-catching visuals for digital and print media, including branding materials, social media graphics, and marketing content. You will collaborate with our team to ensure high-quality designs that align with our brand identity. If you have a passion for design, a keen eye for detail, and the ability to bring ideas to life, we’d love to have you on board!

Responsibility

    Responsibilities:
  • Create visually appealing designs for digital and print media.
  • Develop branding materials, social media graphics, and marketing content.
  • Collaborate with the team to meet design requirements.
  • Ensure consistency in design across all platforms.
  • updated with design trends and tools.

Qualifications

  • Proficiency in design software (Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, etc.).
  • Strong creativity and attention to detail.
  • Ability to work on multiple projects and meet deadlines.
  • Good communication and teamwork skills.
  • Experience in Web3 or crypto-related designs (a plus).

Benefits

Competitive salary and flexible working hours. Opportunity to work on creative and exciting projects. Professional growth and learning opportunities. Supportive and collaborative work environment. Remote work options available. Join us and bring your creativity to life!
